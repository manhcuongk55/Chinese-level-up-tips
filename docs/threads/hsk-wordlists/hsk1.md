# 📗 HSK 1 Vocabulary — 150 Essential Words

> These 150 words form the foundation of HSK Level 1.
> Master these and you can handle basic greetings, numbers, and simple conversations.

---

## How to Use This List

- **Pinyin**: The romanized pronunciation
- **Tone**: 1 = flat ā, 2 = rising á, 3 = dip ǎ, 4 = falling à
- **Study tip**: Don't memorize lists — learn each word *in a sentence*!
