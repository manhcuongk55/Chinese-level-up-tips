## 🌏 Why Learn Chinese?

Chinese (Mandarin) is spoken by **1.4 billion people** and is the gateway to:

- 🏯 **Ancient & modern culture** — 5,000 years of history, philosophy, art & food
- ✈️ **Travel** — Navigate China, Taiwan, Singapore, and Chinese communities worldwide with ease
- 💼 **Career opportunities** — The world's 2nd largest economy
- 🎬 **Entertainment** — Dramas, films, music, and literature in their original form
- 🧠 **Brain health** — Learning a tonal language improves memory, focus, and creativity

> **语言是通往另一个世界的钥匙。**
> *Language is the key to another world.* — A Chinese proverb

## 🚀 What is This Guide?

An **open-source, practical, science-backed** guide to mastering Mandarin — from zero to HSK 6. Inspired by [byoungd/English-level-up-tips](https://github.com/byoungd/English-level-up-tips).

- ✅ **Free forever** — No paywalls, no subscriptions
- ✅ **For all learners** — Works for English speakers and any language background
- ✅ **Structured** — Clear HSK-aligned roadmap with milestones
- ✅ **Community-driven** — You can contribute, translate, and improve it

## 🗺️ HSK Roadmap

```
🌱 HSK 1 (150 words)      →  Basic greetings, numbers, daily survival
🌿 HSK 2 (300 words)      →  Simple sentences, shopping, family
🌳 HSK 3 (600 words)      →  Everyday conversations, short readings
🎋 HSK 4 (1,200 words)    →  Fluent discussions, understanding dramas
🏔️ HSK 5 (2,500 words)    →  Professional settings, Chinese films raw
🏆 HSK 6 (5,000+ words)   →  Near-native, academic Chinese
```

## 🌍 Culture & Travel Bonus

Learning Chinese opens doors to:

| Destination | Why Chinese Helps |
|-------------|------------------|
| 🇨🇳 Mainland China | Navigate cities, read menus, connect with locals |
| 🇹🇼 Taiwan | Traditional characters, Mandarin + local culture |
| 🇸🇬 Singapore | Mandarin is an official language |
| 🇭🇰 Hong Kong | Cantonese dominant, but Mandarin widely understood |
| 🌐 Chinatowns worldwide | Connect with diaspora communities |

> 💡 **Travel tip**: Even basic HSK 2 Chinese will transform your travel experience in China from tourist to traveler.
